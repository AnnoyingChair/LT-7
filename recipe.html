Omelet wrap with Cucumber salad- Aaila bint-Aamir

Ingredients

  • 1 wholemeal gluten free wrap
  • ¼ capsicum -high in vitamin C
  • ¼ red onion -good source of antioxidants
  • 1/2 cup spinach leaves-high in iron
  • 4 cherry tomatoes
  • 1tbs Mayo


  • ½ cucumber-Source of Potassium, Magnesium and Manganese
  • 2 cherry tomatoes
  • olive oil
  • 1/2 carrot
  • 1/2 cup spinach leaves
  • pepper
  • 3 cos lettuce leaves-high in vitamin A

Method

    Omelet Wrap

  1. Wash hands and wear appropriate PPE
  2. Gather the equipment on the bench
  3. Dice the tomatoes, red onion
  4. Finely slice the mushrooms
  5. Crack the eggs into a medium sized bowl and whisk until fluffy 6.Pour the diced and Sliced vegetables and spinach into the eggs
  6. Mix the omelet mixture thoroughly
  7. Heat the electric fry-pan until warm on medium-low
  8. Pour enough oil to cook the omelet
  9. Pour the omelet mixture into the electric-fry-pan
  10. Wait a minute before flipping it over carefully with a spatula
  11. Let the other side cook until the egg has mostly solidified
  12. Place your wholemeal omelet wrap on a plate and spread mayo around evenly.
  13. Fold the bottom, roll the wrap and lastly fold down the top, or roll the wrap as you wish
  14. Cut in half and Place the two halves in a plastic container


    Salad

  1. Grab a green chopping board and knife
  2. Slice the cucumber in half, and then start cutting at normal to make semicircle shapes
  3. Put the cut cucumbers in a medium bowl
  4. Cut the carrots into matchsticks and place them in bowl as wel
  5. Chop the lettuce
  6. Add the lettuce, spinach and cherry tomatoes to the bowl
  7. Season with the pepper, olive oil and a pinch of salt
  8. Mix the salad thoroughly
  9. Put the salad next to the wraps in the plastic container
